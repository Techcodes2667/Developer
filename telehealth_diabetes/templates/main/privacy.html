Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Last updated: December 30, 2024

Introduction

DiabetesCare ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our telehealth diabetes care platform.

We are fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Emergency contact information
  • Insurance information and billing details
  • Account credentials and preferences
Health Information
  • Diabetes type and diagnosis date
  • Blood glucose readings and trends
  • Medication information and adherence
  • Blood pressure, weight, and other vital signs
  • Appointment notes and treatment plans
  • Mental health assessments and mood tracking
Technical Information
  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage patterns and platform interactions
  • Log files and error reports

How We Use Your Information

Healthcare Services
  • Provide telehealth consultations
  • Track health progress
  • Generate health reports
  • Medication management
Platform Operations
  • Account management
  • Technical support
  • Platform improvements
  • Security monitoring
Communications
  • Appointment reminders
  • Health alerts
  • Educational content
  • Platform updates
Analytics
  • Usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Platform performance
  • Research (with consent)
  • Quality improvement

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell your personal health information.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Healthcare Providers

With your healthcare providers involved in your care, including doctors, nurses, and specialists within our network.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or to protect public health and safety.

Business Associates

With HIPAA-compliant service providers who help us operate our platform (cloud hosting, payment processing, etc.).

Emergency Situations

With emergency contacts or healthcare providers in case of medical emergencies.

Data Security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.

Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring and regular security audits protect against threats.

Backups

Regular encrypted backups ensure your data is never lost.

Your Privacy Rights

Access Your Data

Request a copy of your personal health information.

Correct Your Data

Request corrections to inaccurate information.

Restrict Processing

Request limitations on how we use your information.

Data Portability

Export your data in a machine-readable format.

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your account and data.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for optional data processing.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal or medical record-keeping requirements even after account deletion.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage (anonymized)
  • Improve platform performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for users 13 years and older. For users under 18, parental consent is required. We take special care to protect the privacy of minors and comply with applicable children's privacy laws.

International Users

If you are accessing our platform from outside Kenya, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in Kenya. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a prominent notice on your dashboard

Your continued use of our platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
