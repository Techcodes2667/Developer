Understanding telehealth technology and its benefits for diabetes management in Kisumu and beyond

What is Telehealth?

Telehealth uses digital technology to deliver healthcare services remotely. It enables patients to receive medical care, education, and support from healthcare professionals without the need for in-person visits.

For diabetes management, telehealth provides continuous monitoring, real-time consultations, and personalized care plans that help patients maintain better control of their condition.
