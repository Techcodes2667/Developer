Cooking Tips for Diabetes

Choose Whole Grains

Opt for brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat pasta for better blood sugar control.

Load Up on Vegetables

Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables for fiber and nutrients.

Use Healthy Fats

Include olive oil, avocados, and nuts for heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.

Watch Portion Sizes

Use the plate method: 1/2 vegetables, 1/4 protein, 1/4 healthy carbs.
