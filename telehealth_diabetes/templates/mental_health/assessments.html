About Mental Health Assessments

These self-assessment tools are designed to help you understand your mental health better. They are not diagnostic tools and should not replace professional medical advice.

Benefits of Regular Assessment:
  • Track your mental health over time
  • Identify patterns and triggers
  • Better communicate with healthcare providers
  • Monitor progress in treatment
Important Notes:
  • Results are confidential and secure
  • Take assessments in a quiet, private space
  • Answer honestly for accurate results
  • Consult professionals for concerning results
